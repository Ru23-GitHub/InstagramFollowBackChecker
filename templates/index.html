How To Use
Step 1

Click settings button on profile

Step 2

Click "Privacy and Security"

Step 3

Click "Request Download"

Step 4

Enter email, SELECT JSON (IMPORTANT), continue

Step 5

Download information from email when it arrives (usually in 48hrs)

Step 6

Open file, open "followers_and_following"

Step 7

Find "followers.json" and "following.json." These are the files you'll submit on this site.
